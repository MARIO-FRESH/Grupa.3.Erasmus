About Us

Welcome to Tres Culturas, where the flavors of Spain, Romania, and Italy blend perfectly to create a unique culinary experience. Discover traditional dishes prepared with passion and authentic ingredients.

Our Mission

At Tres Culturas, we aim to bring the authenticity of traditional cuisines directly to your plate, offering authentic dishes and the finest quality ingredients.


Our Values

We believe in passion, quality, and hospitality. We are committed to providing an unforgettable culinary experience in a warm and welcoming atmosphere.


Our History

The restaurant was founded out of love for gastronomy and the desire to share traditional recipes. Over the years, we have become a meeting place for food lovers.


House Specialties

Among our most appreciated dishes are Spanish paella, Romanian sarmale, and homemade pasta made from Italian recipes.
